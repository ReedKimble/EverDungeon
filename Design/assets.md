# Assets

All assets are AI generated and meant to be simplistic. Blocky characters and objects are preferred. Simple consistent looking models are preferred to a mismatch of prefabs from various sources. Whatever model style can be generated consistently and accurately across models from races to creatures to objects and items should be preferred.

Coloring can be vivid on creatures and items to contrast bleak surroundings of caverns and dungeons. Lighting should be used extensively since we will have limited assets on screen at one time with limited draw distances since everything is underground in caves and caverns.

Graphic fidelity is not important for this game - it just needs to be obvious that one creature is different from another and that the factions are distinct through color and accessories.
